Score Breakdown Explained

Lag Penalty

Every 1k messages of sustained consumer lag costs you −0.6 points/sec. Hot partitions (top 10% lag) cost 3× as much. Reset by closing the watermark gap.

Fairness Bonus

Jain's Fairness Index over consumer loads × 200, multiplied by wave tier. Reward is exponential — going from 70% → 90% fairness nearly doubles the bonus.

Speed Bonus

Rebalance settlement time < 3s gives full +500. Every extra second halves it. Cooperative strategies get a 1.3× multiplier for zero-downtime.

Checkpoint Bonus

Each commit persists N offsets for +15 × N. Committing during high lag (>50% cap) gives a 2× clutch multiplier.

Failure Handled

Each broker failure you recover from (no lost partitions, < 15s settle) is worth +1200. Silent failures — where lag spikes past 2× cap — cost you −800.

Multiplier

Final score × wave tier × strategy modifier. A level-6 cooperative-sticky run can 3× your total. Keep the combo going by surviving consecutive waves without emergency rollback.

How To Coordinate

A six-step primer on running the arena. Read once, play forever. Hotkeys are listed at the bottom of this page.

Onboarding
1

Press START

The arena boots with 4 consumers and 8 partitions assigned round-robin across 3 brokers. Producers begin emitting at baseline throughput — easy difficulty.

2

Watch the lag

The top-right Lag pill turns warm→red as consumer offsets fall behind partition high-watermark. Hot partitions glow in the consumer bays.

3

Drag partitions

Drag a hot partition from an overloaded consumer onto an idle one. The assignor commits the move immediately — cooperative mode avoids stop-the-world.

4

Checkpoint often

Press Space or the big amber button to spend action budget on an offset commit. Budget regens +1 every 3 seconds up to 10.

5

Survive broker failure

Every 20–40s a broker goes dark. Affected partitions appear on surviving brokers via ISR election — but their consumers may be gone. Reassign fast.

6

Level up

Each level ups producer pressure by 35% and reduces your action budget regen by 0.1s. Survive 5 waves to advance. The best coordinators reach level 8.
